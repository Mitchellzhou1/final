Alice Jordan

Man, being glued to video games took me to some dark places. It started as harmless fun, you know? But then, it turned into this black hole I couldn’t escape. I'd spend days locked away, lost in virtual worlds while reality slipped by. Friends faded away, and the only victories I knew were digital. It was this suffocating cycle—playing to escape, but feeling more trapped each day. There were moments when I felt utterly alone, surrounded by screens but completely isolated. It took hitting rock bottom to realize I needed to step back, find joy in the real world, and rebuild what I'd lost to the pixels. It was a tough journey, but slowly, I found my way back, finding balance and reconnecting with the world beyond the games.
