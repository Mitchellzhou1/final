The Table Church

The Table Church is a local community church in Chinatown NYC. They offer spiritual guidance, counseling, and a supportive community for individuals struggling with addiction. This can provide a sense of belonging and purpose, aiding in recovery.

SAMHSA

SAMHSA stands for the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration. While they primarily focus on substance abuse and mental health, they also address behavioral addictions like internet addiction.

NYC Health

NYC Health is the New York City government's public health agency, responsible for promoting and protecting the health of all New Yorkers. While NYC Health focuses on various health concerns, they also address behavioral health issues, including internet addiction, within their broader public health initiatives.
